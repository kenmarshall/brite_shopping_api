Brite Shopping

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

Brite Shopping ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free grocery price comparison tool for Jamaican shoppers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Anonymous Device Identifier

When you first open the app, a random unique identifier (UUID) is generated on your device. This ID is used solely to associate your shopping list with your device. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any other personal information.

Shopping List Data

Your shopping list is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you choose to sync your list, it is stored on our servers linked only to your anonymous device ID. No personal information is associated with your list.

Barcode Scans

When you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to our servers to look up product and price information. We may store barcode lookups to improve our product catalog. No images from your camera are stored or transmitted.

Search Queries

Product search queries are processed on our servers to return results. We do not log or store individual search queries linked to any user or device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photos or camera images
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Advertising identifiers

No Account Required

Brite Shopping does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information. You can use all features of the app anonymously.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising, analytics, or any other commercial purpose. Our servers communicate only with our own database to provide product and price information.

The app uses Open Food Facts, a public and open database, to look up barcode information. Barcode queries to Open Food Facts are anonymous. See their privacy policy at openfoodfacts.org/privacy.

Data Storage & Security

Shopping list data and anonymous device identifiers are stored on MongoDB Atlas, a cloud database service with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorized application servers only.

Children's Privacy

Brite Shopping does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information from any user, there is no special risk to children using the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at support@briteshopping.com.
